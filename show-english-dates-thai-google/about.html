Show English dates on Thai Google search results

This extension converts Thai Buddhist calendar years to Western (Gregorian) years and Thai month names/abbreviations to English on Google pages.

Created by Ken Nowak — roarpub.com/about-ken-nowak/

Version 1.0.3 • Built 2025-11-11

If you installed as an unpacked extension, manage it at chrome://extensions. This page appears under “Extension options”.
